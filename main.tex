\begin{column}{0.48\textwidth}
\begin{block}{How to read the state}
\begin{itemize}\tightitems
  \item \key{High capacity + fast resiliency:} raise FAK confidence.
  \item \key{High cost or toxicity:} revise fill expectation and aggression.
  \item \key{High internalization:} match internally before external routing.
  \item \key{Weak state:} delay, slice smaller, or hedge earlier.
\end{itemize}
\end{block}
\vspace{0.25em}
\begin{block}{Design principle}
\begin{center}
{\bfseries Price is the observation. Liquidity is the control state.}
\end{center}
\end{block}
